Overall informations

The site contains a large number of calculators which based mostly on Eurocodes with Polish national annexes.

Snow load calculators

Mostly based on Eurocode 1 - Actions on structures Part 1-3: General actions - Snow loads with Polish national annex. This calculator contains all possible design situations and roof types which are described in the document. This calculators shouldn't be used for or doesn't have informations how to calculate:

  • sites at altitudes above 1 500m,
  • impact snow loads resulting from sliding off or falling from a higher roof,
  • additional wind loads which could result from changes in shape or size of the construction works due to the presence of snow or the accretion of ice,
  • loads in areas where snow is present all year round,
  • ice loading,
  • lateral loading due to snow (e.g. lateral loads exerted by drifts),
  • snow loads on bridges.

Dead load calculator

Mostly based on Eurocode 1 - Actions on structures Part 1-1: General actions - Densities, self-weight, imposed loads for buildings with Polish national annex and on Polish standard document PN-82/B-02001 Building loads - permanent loads which contains informations about the density of the building materials. This calculator allows us to get the information about the weight of all layers in the structure.
